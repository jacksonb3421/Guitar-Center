Guitar Lessons in Kalispell, MT

Looking to learn guitar in the Flathead Valley? Our professional instructors in Kalispell, Montana offer personalized lessons for all ages and skill levels. Whether you're picking up a guitar for the first time or looking to sharpen your technique, we've got you covered.

Beginner Lessons

Start your guitar journey with the basics: tuning, simple chords, and strumming. Perfect for all ages with no prior experience.

Available: Weekdays & Saturdays

Intermediate Training

Improve your technique, learn barre chords, scales, and explore music theory. Ideal for those with basic guitar knowledge.

Available: Evenings & Weekends

Advanced Coaching

Refine your skills with advanced improvisation, soloing, and genre-specific styles including blues, rock, and jazz.

Instructor: Local touring professionals

One-on-One Private Sessions

Get tailored instruction in a quiet, focused environment. Lessons held in Kalispell or virtually via Zoom.

Pricing: Starting at $35 per session

Location

We are based in downtown Kalispell, with convenient access for students coming from Whitefish, Columbia Falls, and Bigfork.

How to Sign Up

Contact us via our Contact Page to schedule your first lesson or get matched with the right instructor based on your goals and experience.
