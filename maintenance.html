Guitar Maintenance

Keep your guitar in top shape with these essential maintenance tips:

  • Regularly clean the fretboard and body with a soft cloth.
  • Change strings every 1-3 months depending on play frequency.
  • Check and adjust the guitar's neck relief using the truss rod.
  • Keep the guitar stored in a controlled environment, away from extreme temperatures and humidity.
  • Use proper guitar cases or stands for safe storage.
